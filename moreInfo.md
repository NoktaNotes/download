## About Nokta
Nokta is a personal project, my attempt at creating my own perfect note app. Clean, fast, and no clutter. I wanted something simple: a straightforwared editor, flexible note organization, and no unnecessary features getting in the way. Nokta is the result.
This is a one-person project, developed in my spare time. I use it daily myself, which is the best motivation to keep improving it.

### Version & Feedback
Nokta is currently at version 0.10. It's functional and stable for daily use, but still evolving. If you encounter bugs, have suggestions, or just want to share your thoughts, please open an issue on GitHub.

### Privacy
Nokta stores all data locally on your device. No data is collected or sent anywhere.
